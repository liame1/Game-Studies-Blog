Blog Post 6:

4/13/2026

Title of game: Milestone Credits: Artist/Designer - Liam Esler Programmer/Producer - Rongjie Mai Known Issues: Lack of animations, Buggy platforms, How to play: Use WASD for movement, and SPACEBAR to jump, try to reach the blue objective while avoiding the enemies. Reflection of Game Dev Process: Both my teammate and I lost the passion for this project midway through development. It felt like more of a chore than a something exciting. Our main issue was that we went in blind, overscoped the idea, and felt the need to finish this project. In hindsight, we should have pivoted from the start to a more simple and concrete Idea. When working on the character and background, I likely spent upwards of several hours. I wanted to create something that would impress myself. I don't regret this choice, but it was not practical to continue with this art style given how much more time and energy I would have to devote if I wanted to finish all of the required assets before the project deadline. The positive takeaway is that I will be able to scope better for future projects, the idea I have in mind now is much simpler.
